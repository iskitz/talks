Michael Lee, Creator, Ajile

Michael Lee is the creator of the Ajile JavaScript Library and a Solutions Engineer at Canon U.S.A., Inc. At Canon he product manages all locally created embedded multi-functional software solutions and is the creator of Canon's flagship Authorized Send solution.

Michael has been captivated by JavaScript and Web Development for over ten years and constantly works to discover and enhance its capabilities. He is a forward thinker currently focused on extending Web Development to create more automated and intuitive systems to enhance the human experience.
